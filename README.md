### 👨‍💻 About Me

I am an Electrical and Electronic Engineering student with a deep passion for Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Hardware design. I enjoy building interactive educational tools and visualizations that demystify complex engineering and AI concepts. Driven by the belief that learning should be intuitive, visual, and hands-on rather than strictly theoretical, my goal is to bridge the gap between AI and engineering education to solve real-world problems effectively.
